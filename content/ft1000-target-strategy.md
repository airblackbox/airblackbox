## The Pitch Template (FT1000-Specific)

Different from the open-source scan pitch. These are COMMERCIAL companies, not open-source projects. The approach:

**Subject**: EU AI Act compliance for [Company] (FT1000 recognition + August 2026 deadline)

**Hook**: "Congratulations on the FT1000 recognition. As one of Europe's fastest-growing [AI/tech] companies, you're exactly the kind of business the EU AI Act was designed for."

**The ask**: Offer a free compliance scan of any Python codebase they want to point us at. Not a public GitHub scan (these are commercial companies, likely closed-source).

**The close**: "The scanner runs entirely on your infrastructure. Zero data leaves your machine. We can do a 30-minute walkthrough of what the scan finds and what it means for your August 2026 deadline."

## Key Differences from Open-Source Outreach

| | Open-Source Targets | FT1000 Targets |
|---|---|---|
| **Repo access** | Public GitHub, we scan first | Closed-source, offer to scan |
| **Proof point** | "Here are your results" | "Here's what we found scanning 8 frameworks like yours" |
| **Decision maker** | Founder/maintainer | CTO, Head of Engineering, or Head of Compliance |
| **Value prop** | Community credibility + compliance | Enterprise risk + investor confidence |
| **Price point** | Free scanner → $299 VSP | Free scan → VSP → potential enterprise deal |
| **Urgency** | August 2026 deadline | FT1000 badge + deadline = reputation at stake |
